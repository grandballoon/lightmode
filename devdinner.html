Dev Dinners are a chance to effectively learn programming, from the level of a complete beginner, in a relaxed dinner party environment.

Dev Dinners are one of Lightmode's Pilots for Summer of 2025.

The first dinner is Friday, June 20th, at 7pm in the Bushwick neighborhood of Brooklyn (ticket link below). These will run throughout Summer 2025.

Over the course of these dinners, we’ll go through the history, theory, and practice of programming through a wide variety of lenses designed to make coding intuitively accessible even (and especially) to people who were told they were “bad at math.”
