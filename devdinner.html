Dev Dinners are a chance to effectively learn programming, from the level of a complete beginner, in a relaxed dinner party environment. Dev Dinners are part of Lightmode's Pilot Season for Summer of 2025.

Lightmode is running Dev Dinners throughout Summer 2026. The first one is Friday, June 20th, at 7pm in the Bushwick neighborhood of Brooklyn (ticket link below).

Over the course of these dinners, we’ll go through the history, theory, and practice of programming through a wide variety of lenses designed to make coding intuitively accessible even (and especially) to people who were told they were “bad at math.”
